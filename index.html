Best Education

Simulation is one of the most important training methods that managers rely on to rationalize costs. Not only rationalizing financial costs, but also the cost of time and human effort. The simulation training method is also relied upon to achieve an advanced level of quality as the trainee begins to enter the actual work environment, without relying on the principle of raising the quality of performance through right and wrong, which undoubtedly affects the reputation of the employee and the organization.

Visual Reality

Virtual reality (VR) is a simulated experience that employs pose tracking and 3D near-eye displays to give the user an immersive feel of a virtual world. Applications of virtual reality include entertainment (particularly video games), education (such as medical or military training) and business (such as virtual meetings).

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality is an interactive experience that enhances the real world with computer-generated perceptual information. Using software, apps, and hardware such as AR glasses, augmented reality overlays digital content onto real-life environments and objects.
